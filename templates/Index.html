The entry zone between outside and inside. Soul material on a software surface — warm metals, hairlines, sharp corners. For marketing pages, in-product UI, portfolios, anything digital but soul-leaning.

Hiroma register

hiroma

The great room. The synthesis register — soul prose, genkan structure, cockpit instrumentation, orchestrated in one composition. Reserved for the artifacts that need all three voices in the same breath: studio pages, case studies, capability briefs, annual reports.

Evening register

evening

Lamp-lit. Aubergine and inkwater. For sustained, slow, private artifacts read at the end of the day — chapbooks, prayers, letters to the body, anything where the page must hold the subject without flinching. One column, narrow measure, long fall.

— 13

Evening chapbook · five movements · aubergine
A designer's evening prayer. Cartouche, frontispiece, five sustained movements with held-note credos and minimal caesurae, colophon. Cardo italic display on aubergine, champagne the held note.

Ophelia
Sustained prose · letter · inkwater
A letter to the body, in five movements. Vertical sideways marginalia, drowned-rouge accent, petal-white for the line that must be looked at. The same evening discipline, the opposite tone from Vesper.
